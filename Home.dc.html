01 — The Academy

CRICKET IS TAUGHT HERE LIKE IT MATTERS — BECAUSE IN RANCHI, IT DOES.

RPCA was founded to give the young cricketers of Ranchi and greater Jharkhand a place to train the right way — structured coaching, honest feedback, and the patience to build players over seasons, not weekends. From a child gripping a bat for the first time to a state-level aspirant chasing selection, every player follows a clear pathway.

We keep batch sizes disciplined, coach ratios tight, and standards high. This is the city that gave Indian cricket one of its greatest captains — our job is to make sure the next generation is ready when their chance comes.
