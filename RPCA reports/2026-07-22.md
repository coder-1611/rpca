view, two of the reviewers are:

- **"shalini soni"** — matches the Google account that manages this profile

Reviews written by the business, its staff, or its owner's own accounts breach Google's
[prohibited content policy](https://support.google.com/contributionpolicy/answer/7400114) on
conflicts of interest. Google's filters detect them — usually in a batch, exactly as happened here —
and remove them. That is the most probable explanation for 15 → 6.

**Why this matters more than any ranking tweak:** repeated policy-violating reviews escalate from
"reviews removed" to **profile suspension**. A suspended profile cannot appear in the map pack at
all, which would end the campaign rather than slow it. The reviews that survive filtering are the
ones from real students and parents on their own accounts, from their own devices.

**Recommended:** stop adding reviews from academy-connected accounts, and send the genuine ask in
`seo/whatsapp-review-request.md` to the parents/players group instead. 30–40 real reviews beats 300
filtered ones, and it's the only version that actually moves the map pack.
