# WhatsApp review-request message

Send to the parents/players group. Replace `<REVIEW_LINK>` with the short link from
Google Business Profile → **Ask for reviews** (business.google.com, already logged in).

---

**English:**

Namaste! 🏏 We're setting up RPCA's Google page so more young cricketers in Ranchi can find the academy. If your time at RPCA has meant something to you or your child, a 2-minute Google review would help us a lot:

<REVIEW_LINK>

A line or two about what you train for — batting, bowling, the coaches, the ground — is perfect. Thank you! — Team RPCA

---

**Hinglish (often converts better in the group):**

Namaste! 🏏 Hum RPCA ka Google page set kar rahe hain taaki Ranchi ke aur bhi young cricketers academy tak pahunch sakein. Agar aapko ya aapke bachche ko RPCA mein training ka accha experience raha hai, toh 2 minute ka Google review humare liye bahut helpful hoga:

<REVIEW_LINK>

Bas 1–2 line likhiye — coaching, nets, coaches, ground — jo bhi aapko accha laga. Dhanyavaad! — Team RPCA

---

Notes:
- Do NOT offer anything in exchange for reviews (against Google policy, can get reviews filtered).
- Ask once, then remind once after ~10 days. Reply to every review from the GBP account.
