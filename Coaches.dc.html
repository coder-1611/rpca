Ashutosh Mishra
FOUNDER & HEAD COACH

ASHUTOSH
MISHRA

"We don't chase quick results. We build cricketers who are still improving three seasons from now."

Ashutosh is the founder and head coach of RPCA — a Ranchi cricketer who built the academy to develop local Jharkhand talent the right way. His coaching is discipline-first and hands-on: long, focused hours in the nets, honest correction, and a patient, long-term approach to every player's development.

He sets the standards the academy runs on — punctuality, effort and respect for the game — and stays personally involved in the progress of players across every batch, from a child's first session to a senior chasing selection.

YUVRAJ
KUMAR

"I'm still in the middle every season — so what I coach is what actually works out there."

Yuvraj is a young all-rounder from Jharkhand, registered with the Jharkhand State Cricket Association (JSCA), who brings current competitive match experience straight into the RPCA nets. Because he's still playing at a serious level, his coaching stays sharp and practical — the habits, plans and temperament that hold up under real match pressure.

He works closely with junior and senior batches on all-round skills — batting intent, bowling to a plan, and the fielding and fitness that modern cricket demands — helping players bridge the gap between practice and performance.

Yuvraj Kumar
ALL-ROUNDER · JSCA
Our Coaching Philosophy

Great cricketers aren't rushed into shape. They're built — session by session, correction by correction — on sound technique, honest feedback, and the discipline to keep showing up. At RPCA, we coach the player in front of us, not a template. Small batches, close attention, and a plan that runs over seasons, not weeks.
