RPCA — 01 · Programs

The Pathway.

One delivery, three stages — a structured route from a child's first grip to a cricketer knocking on the door of state selection.

How we build players

We don't sell net time. We build a player over years — stage by stage, with the next step always in view.

Batch sizes

Small groups so every player gets real, honest correction each session — not a queue.

Sessions

Morning and evening batches, so school and work don't have to mean missing training.

Progression

Move up when your game is ready — assessed by your coach, not by your birthday alone.

The goal

District, JSCA age-group and league cricket — a real, measured destination.

Foundation · Ages 7–12
Stage 01

Foundation

The most important years. Before a single statistic matters, we build a clean, repeatable technique and — just as importantly — a love for the game that survives the hard days.

  • 01

    Grip, Stance & Balance

    The non-negotiable fundamentals, drilled until they hold without thinking.

  • 02

    Soft-ball to Hard-ball

    A safe, confidence-building progression onto the real cricket ball.

  • 03

    Habits First

    Punctuality, effort and respect — the things that make cricketers before runs do.

Development · Ages 13–16
Stage 02

Development

Technique meets pressure. This is where a decent player becomes a dependable one — able to read a length, build an innings and hold a spell when it matters.

  • 01

    Bowling Machine Work

    Programmable pace and length to groove a shot or expose and drill a weakness.

  • 02

    Video Analysis

    Slow-motion breakdowns — see the flaw, then fix it inside the same session.

  • 03

    Match-Simulation Nets

    Scenario training: chase a target, defend an over, bowl at the death.

Elite Pathway · Ages 17+
Stage 03

Elite Pathway

The state route. For players chasing real cricket, we prepare the whole athlete — technique, tactics, strength and temperament — for JSCA trials and competitive league cricket.

RPCA players have gone on to represent Ranchi sides in the Jharkhand T20.

  • 01

    Strength & Conditioning

    Building the engine competitive cricket actually demands, injury-smart.

  • 02

    Competitive Fixtures

    Real match exposure to test the work against opposition that pushes back.

  • 03

    Trial Preparation

    Targeted readiness for JSCA age-group and league selection.

Group Batches

Focused

One-on-One
